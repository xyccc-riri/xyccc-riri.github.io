$例 2: \lim \limits_{x \rightarrow 0}[1+\ln (1+x)]^{\frac{2}{x}} $
$例 3: \lim \limits_{x \rightarrow 0}\left(x \sin \frac{1}{x}+\frac{1}{x} \sin x\right) $
$例 4: \lim \limits_{x \rightarrow \infty}\left(1+\frac{2}{x}\right)^{-k x}=e^{-2} , 求 k 的值$

\frac{\ln (1+x)}{x} & x>0 \\
e^{-x} & x \leq 0
